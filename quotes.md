A running collection of words that stuck.

---

> "Blackbird singing in the dead of night  
> Take these broken wings and learn to fly  
> All your life  
> You were only waiting for this moment to arise."

<p style="text-align: right; font-style: italic;">— The Beatles, Blackbird</p>

---

> "All we have to decide is what to do with the time that is given us."

<p style="text-align: right; font-style: italic;">— J.R.R. Tolkien, The Fellowship of the Ring</p>

---

> "Do not go gentle into that good night,  
> Old age should burn and rave at close of day;  
> Rage, rage against the dying of the light."

<p style="text-align: right; font-style: italic;">— Dylan Thomas, Do Not Go Gentle Into That Good Night</p>

---

> "And when the night is cloudy, there is still a light that shines on me, shine until tomorrow, let it be."

<p style="text-align: right; font-style: italic;">— The Beatles, Let It Be</p>
